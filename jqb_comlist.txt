Hello, fellow user. Here's all the commands (sort by categories):
### 1. Utility:
_help - Shows the help guide to the bot.
_say <arg> - Make the bot say something.
_name [name] - Changes the name of the bot.

_dfm-rn - Shows the current song and artist playing on derp.fm.

_unixtime - Shows the time in Unix format.
_igv <number> - Shows the IGV/VAT of the base amount with the next formula: ((num/100)*18)
_bigv <number> - Shows the base amount of the price with the formula: (num/1.18)
_celf <number> - Converts Celsius into Fahrenheit.
_fahc <number> - Converts Fahrenheit into Celsius.
_celk <number> - Converts Celsius into Kelvin.
_fahk <number> - Converst Fahrenheit into Kelvin.
_kmmi <number> - Converts kilometers into miles.
_mikm <number> - Converts miles into kilometers.
_room [roomname] - Makes the bot go to a room.
_save <filename> <content> - Saves a file.
_load <filename> - Loads a file.
_hostweather - Shows host's weather info.
_weather <location> - Shows weather from location. //it has graphics unlike dudebot
